    Cancellation of Missed Class / No Show

    As a courtesy to our staff & guests, we require at least 5 hours notice when canceling a private or semi-private appointment or group class. It is your responsibility to notify Options Studio by phone, SMS or online, at least 5 hours in advance, should you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, to avoid incurring a cancellation fee. Any appointment cancelled or missed with less than 5 hour-notice will result in a charge or loss of that session fee.

    To avoid delaying the next student, your class will finish on time regardless of how late you begin.
